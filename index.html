◈ 02 — About

How it Works

PUDRUS.XYZ is a browser-based creative suite for image and video processing. All tools run locally on your machine. Your files are never uploaded to a server, ensuring 100% privacy.

Built with vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no accounts. Just pure, fast, creative processing.
